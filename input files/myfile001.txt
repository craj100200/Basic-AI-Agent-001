[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Introduction to AI[TITLE_END]
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence.
It includes problem-solving, learning, and reasoning.
AI can be categorized as Narrow AI, General AI, and Super AI.
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Applications of AI[TITLE_END]
Self-driving cars
Medical diagnosis
Personal assistants like Siri and Alexa
Recommendation systems (Netflix, Amazon)
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Machine Learning[TITLE_END]
Machine Learning is a subset of AI.
It allows systems to learn from data without explicit programming.
Types: Supervised, Unsupervised, Reinforcement learning.
[SLIDE_END]

[SLIDE_START]
[TITLE_START]Conclusion[TITLE_END]
AI is transforming industries and daily life.
Ethical considerations and safety are important.
Continuous learning and research drive AI progress.
[SLIDE_END]
